Frequently Asked Questions

What makes PW Power Batches different from regular batches?
PW Power Batches offer small group learning (limited to ~100 students), two-way live interaction, personalized doubt resolution, dedicated mentors, comprehensive test series, and performance tracking. The focus is on quality teaching with individual attention.
How do I apply the discount coupon during enrollment?
After receiving your coupon code via email, proceed to the PW Power Batch enrollment page. During checkout, you'll find an "Offers & Discounts" section where you can enter your coupon code. The discount will be automatically applied to your total fee.
What are the payment options available?
You can choose between:
  • Full payment (with maximum discount)
  • Installment option by PW (auto debit, no documentation needed)
  • EMI finance option through Simplify
For most 1-year batches, you can pay ₹500 to book your seat and the remaining in 2 installments.
Can I attend demo classes before enrolling?
Yes, you can book free demo classes for Power Batches. Visit the PW website, select your course and class, and choose a convenient time slot for the demo. This helps you experience the teaching style before making a commitment.
What if I miss a live class?
All live classes are recorded and made available to students. You can access the recorded lectures, PDF notes, and other study materials anytime through your student dashboard. Additionally, you can clarify doubts through the dedicated doubt support system.
Is there any scholarship or additional discount available?
The ₹4500 coupon discount is currently the best available offer for Power Batches. However, you may check for additional scholarship tests or special promotions that PW occasionally conducts for meritorious students.
